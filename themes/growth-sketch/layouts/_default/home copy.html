The ESP Learning Model

  • Awareness – Learning begins with creating a sense of awareness about the situation.
  • Understanding – This is further enhanced by the resource person, helping the individual gain a greater clarity of their situation.
  • Learning – Understanding facilitates better learning by enabling the individual to see its real-world application.
  • Realisation – As realization unfolds, the individual is now able to perceive their situation in its entirety.
  • Habitualisation – An environment is created within the individual to internalize and habitualize the learned skill or behavior.

FOR BUSINESSES: Tailored Learning Programs

People Developmental Learning (PDL)

"Do you notice a gap in your team’s skillsets? Are they struggling to adapt to new challenges?"

Through PDL, we enhance individual capabilities by nurturing strengths and filling knowledge, skills and practce gaps. Our ESP approach energizes employees to grow and synergize with their teams to collaborate more effectively.

Executives Development Learning (EDL)

"Are your executives facing challenges in leading initiatives or decision-making?"

EDL focuses more on executionary talent-related skills and attitudes. Overall execution excellence and operational skills are the key deliverables.

Management Development Learning (MDL)

"Is your management team struggling to align with company goals or manage departments effectively?"

Managerial productivity is defined by the accomplishment of organizational tactical and strategic goals, no matter the challenges. Linking internal and external customer satisfaction as the highest business priority ensures both bottom-line profitability and growth goals are realized.

Leadership Development Learning (LDL)

"Are your leaders struggling to realize their full potential and inspire others?"

Leadership development helps leaders tackle real-time challenges like disengaged teams and unclear goals. Our program equips them with practical skills in communication, decision-making, and strategic planning to inspire teams and boost productivity. With ESP, leaders can align team efforts with business objectives, ensuring both performance and growth.
